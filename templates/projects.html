Some of my projects:

Goomball

A small adventure game sandbox. Collect keys to open doors and escape the maze. Kick your goomber buddy and put him in jail if you want (mean!)

Made using Phaser JavaScript library.

SpaceX Tracker

Shows recent headlines from the SpaceFlightNews API. Can store favorite images, articles, and more.

Made using HTML/JS, Node/Express/Sequelize, and PostgreSQL. Currently in development.

Insplore

A concept created for the General Assembly team hackathon during my cohort. Experience foreign culture without leaving the safety of your home. (First place of 9 teams!)

Made using Mapbox' API, Bootstrap, and NextJS. We were a 6-person team with 24 hours from theme to finish: I spent almost all of my time working on integrating the API into the browser.

Currently in development.

Projects Showcase

A lightweight app to store, organize, and display your personal projects.

Uses Flask front-end and GraphQL endpoint, as well as mongoDB.

Currently in development.
